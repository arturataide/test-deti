Association DETI is an institution that is headquartered in Geneva, Switzerland and is tax exempted for public utility goal and public-service. Any physical person and any legal entity that make a donation to the Association DETI may deduct the amount of the donation from their tax declaration. Physical persons may deduct up to 20% of their total taxable net income. Legal entities may deduct up to 20% of their taxable net profit. Donations in cash or assets (shares, equipment or real estate assets, debts, intellectual property rights) can be deducted. However, membership fees, mandatory and labour payments are excluded and cannot be deducted.

Association DETI representative must give their donors and sponsors a donation receipt (attestation de dons) when the donation amount exceeds CHF 300.-per year. Please send us an email to info@detinow.ch in case of any questions or to request your donation receipt.
